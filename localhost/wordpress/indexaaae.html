The St. Kitts and Nevis Chamber of Industry and Commerce was incorporated on June 18, 1949 to serve the needs of a post-war, burgeoning local private sector in an economy that was dominated by sugar for over 200 years.The St. Kitts and Nevis Chamber of Industry and Commerce was incorporated on June 18, 1949 to serve the needs of a post-war, burgeoning local private sector in an economy that was dominated by sugar for over 200 years. The organisation functions as a business assistance and research organisation with the mandate to lobby on behalf of its 137 members. The Chamber boasts of a 31-member Nevis Division formed in 1996, and a 13-member Manufacturers’ Division – being the product of a successful amalgamation of the St. Kitts Manufacturers’ Association into our Chamber in 1988.

The following information about our Chamber is intended to aid your understanding of the benefits of membership in our organisation. We trust that you will take advantage of the benefits that membership affords.
